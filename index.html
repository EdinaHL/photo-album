- Málaga Photo Album -

Málaga is the second most populous city in Andaluzia and the 6th most populous in Spain. It's one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Western Europe, founded around 770 BC by the Phoenicians.

The Moors left posterity the dominating presence of the Castle of Gibralfaro, which is connected to the Alcazaba, the lower fortress and royal residence.

Málaga is also the birthplace of many famous people, including Pablo Picasso and Antonio Banderas.
